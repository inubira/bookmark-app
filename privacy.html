Privacy Policy

Yes Sales Bookmarks  |  Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Yes Sales Bookmarks is an internal Chrome extension for Yes Sales employees. It only displays and organizes department bookmarks in your Chrome bookmark bar. It does not perform any other functions.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No information is sent to external servers.

Permissions

The extension requires the bookmarks permission solely to create bookmark folders and links in your Chrome bookmark bar upon installation.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
